Experience

Research Intern, National Institutes of Health

During summer 2018, I worked on computational genetics for osteosarcoma in Dr. Paul Meltzer's lab at the National Cancer Institute.

Software Engineering Intern, Commvault

During summer 2018 and 2017, I worked at Commvault, a company in Tinton Falls, NJ that makes software for enterprise data backup. In 2018, I worked on creating CSV reports and feeding them into an ARIMA model to intelligently schedule background operations to not conflict with times of predicted user activity using C++. In 2017, I worked on creating a dynamic web UI to streamline the process of CI/CD using Angular, Java, and SQL.

Research Intern, Princeton University

During summer and fall 2016, I did research at Princeton's Andlinger Center for Energy and the Environment in Dr. Claire White's sustainable cements group. I analyzed pair distribution function data from the Advanced Photon Source particle accelerator to investigate the effects of sulfate attack on the nanostructure of alkali-activated slag cement, which has 98% fewer CO2 emissions than traditional cement.
